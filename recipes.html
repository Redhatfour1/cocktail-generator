Dirty Martini

Ingredients
  • 6 fluid oz. of vodka
  • 1 dash dry vermouth
  • 1 fluid oz. brine from olive jar
  • 4 stuffed green olives
Method

In a mixing glass, combine vodka, dry vermouth, brine and olives. Pour into a glass over ice. Either drink on the rocks, or strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Bee's Knees

Ingredients
  • 8 oz. gin
  • 4 oz. lemon juice
  • 4 oz. Honey Syrup
  • Lemon peel for garnish
Method

Vigorously shake the gin, simple syrup and lemon juice over ice, and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with the lemon peel.

Campari Spritz

Ingredients
  • 2 fluid oz. of Prosecco
  • 1 fluid oz. of Club Soda
  • 2 oz. of Campari
  • 1 large green olive on a pick
Method

Fill rocks glass with ice. Add sparkling wine, club soda, and Campari. Stir gently. Garnish with olives.

Chrysanthemum

Ingredients
  • 2 fluid oz. of Dry Vermouth
  • 1 fluid oz. of Benedictine
  • 1/4 oz. Absinthe
  • Garnish, orange twist
Method

Fill a mixing glass 2/3 full with ice. Add dry vermouth, Benedictine, and absinthe. Stir until well chilled, about 20 seconds. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with orange twist and serve.
